Legend

Size of a node: Increases with the number of publications
Color of a node: Represents a "Community" (calculated by a modularity algorithm)
Connections between nodes: Represent co-authorship of publications
Width/Strength of a Connection: Represents the number of publications the two authors (nodes) share
Position / Closeness of nodes: Calculated by a force algorithm which aligns the nodes by rules of gravity, depending on their size and connections
